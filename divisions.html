School Divisions

Tailored education for every stage of growth

← Back to Home

Primary School (Ages 5-11)

Our Primary School program builds strong academic foundations while nurturing curiosity and creativity. We focus on literacy, numeracy, and science through engaging, hands-on learning experiences.

Key Features: Small class sizes, play-based learning, specialist subjects, individual attention, character development, arts and music integration

Lower School (Ages 11-14)

Lower School is a time of academic growth and social development. Students deepen subject knowledge while developing research, analytical, and communication skills essential for success.

Key Features: Subject specialization, team mentoring, leadership opportunities, extracurricular enrichment, social-emotional learning, community service

Middle School (Ages 14-16)

Middle School students engage with Cambridge IGCSE curriculum, preparing them for university-level work. We support their growing independence while maintaining strong pastoral care and guidance.

Key Features: Cambridge IGCSE, subject choice flexibility, university preparation, leadership roles, advanced projects, mentorship

Upper School (Ages 16-18)

Our Upper School offers the IB Diploma Programme and Cambridge A-Levels. Students become independent thinkers and leaders, prepared for top universities worldwide.

Key Features: IB Diploma Programme, Cambridge A-Levels, university counseling, internships, extended essays, senior leadership positions
