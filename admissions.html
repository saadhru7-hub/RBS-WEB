Admissions Process

We welcome families interested in exploring Redbridge Academy. Our admissions process is designed to find students who will thrive in our community and benefit from our programs. We assess academic readiness, character, and potential for growth.

1. Enquiry

Contact our admissions team to learn more about Redbridge and schedule a campus tour.

2. Application

Submit the application form with required documents and student information.

3. Assessment

Participate in entrance examinations and interviews appropriate for your grade level.

4. Decision

Receive admissions decision and begin preparations for enrollment.

Application Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school transcripts and report cards
  • Standardized test scores (if applicable)
  • Teacher recommendations
  • Student essay and parent statement
  • Entrance assessments in Math and English
  • Personal interview with admissions staff

School Fees

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When is the admissions deadline?
A: Rolling admissions. Spots fill throughout the year, so early application is recommended.

Q: Do you offer financial aid?
A: Yes, we offer need-based financial assistance. Contact admissions for details.

Q: Can I schedule a campus tour?
A: Absolutely! Tours are available year-round. Contact us to schedule.

Q: What is your international student policy?
A: We welcome international families. Visa support available when applicable.
