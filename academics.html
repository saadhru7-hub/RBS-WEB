Our Approach to Learning

At Redbridge Academy, we believe in creating a balanced educational experience that challenges students intellectually while nurturing their creativity and character. Our rigorous college preparatory curriculum combines classical education with modern innovation.

We use a blend of traditional teaching methods and cutting-edge instructional approaches to foster critical thinking, collaboration, and a passion for lifelong learning.

Curriculum Overview

English & Language Arts

Comprehensive development of communication skills, literary analysis, and writing proficiency across all grade levels.

Mathematics

Problem-solving and critical thinking through innovative math instruction from fundamentals to advanced calculus and beyond.

Science

Hands-on laboratory experiences and inquiry-based learning in biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science.

Social Studies

Global perspectives and historical understanding through the study of cultures, civilizations, and contemporary issues.

World Languages

Fluency development in Spanish, French, Mandarin and other languages with cultural immersion opportunities.

Technology & Computer Science

Digital literacy, coding, and computational thinking integrated throughout the curriculum.

Special Programs

IB Diploma Programme

Internationally recognized two-year program for Upper School students with 98% pass rate and above-world-average points.

Cambridge IGCSE

Rigorous globally recognized qualifications for secondary students ensuring college readiness and academic excellence.

Advanced Placement (AP)

College-level courses allowing students to earn university credit and demonstrate subject mastery.

Our Faculty

Redbridge's greatest asset is our faculty. Our teachers are subject matter experts, experienced educators, and caring mentors who know their students well. With an average of 15:1 student-teacher ratio, teachers can personalize learning for each student's needs and abilities.

Our faculty regularly participate in professional development to stay current with educational best practices and pedagogical innovations.

Academic Results

  • 98% College Acceptance Rate - Our graduates attend leading universities worldwide
  • Above-World-Average IB Points - Consistent excellence in International Baccalaureate results
  • Cambridge Excellence - Outstanding performance in IGCSE and A-Level examinations
  • AP Success - High AP exam scores with students earning college credit
  • College Placements - Graduates accepted to top universities including Cambridge, MIT, Yale, and Stanford
